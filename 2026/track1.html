Introduction

As the wave of artificial intelligence deepens, AI-driven service computing has become a core engine reshaping the digital service ecosystem, integrating advanced AI technologies with service design, delivery, and management to realize intelligent, efficient, and personalized service innovation. This track focuses on the cutting-edge intersection of AI and service computing, aiming to gather global academic achievements and industrial practices, explore solutions to key challenges in intelligent service systems, and promote the innovation and application of intelligent service technologies. We warmly invite researchers, engineers, and practitioners worldwide to submit original research works.

Topics

  • AI-Native Service Modeling and Orchestration
  • Edge AI Service Computing
  • LLM-Powered Service Innovation
  • Intelligent Service Quality & Resource Optimization
  • Industry-Specific AI Service Applications
